Mushroom Soup Porkchops

Ingredients

  • Pork-Chops
  • Mushroom Soup
  • Pasta Noodles of YOUR choice!

Instructions

  1. Fry Pork Chops until golden brown crust
  2. Add Mushroom Soup and bring to a simmer until pork chops are fully cooked
  3. Cook pasta noodles
  4. Cut Pork Chops into bite sized pieces and place over noodles
  5. Add Remaining sauce from pan onto your plate
  6. ENJOY!
