Riceroni Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Riceroni
  • Shrimp or Chicken
  • Cilantro
  • Diced Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Put Riceroni in pan with butter until noodles are golden brown
  2. Add 2 cups of water and add seasoning
  3. bring to a simmer until rice is cooked
  4. Add cooked shrimp and diced tomatoes
  5. Add cilantro to taste
  6. ENJOY!
